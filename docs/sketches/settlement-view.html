Two parallel records, one event

The intercompany invoice (your payable of 75k) and the partner transaction (B's revenue of 75k) represent the same economic event. The invoice tracks your cash obligation — it shows up in your invoices, calendar, and bank balances. The partner transaction tracks the settlement effect — it updates B's balance in the settlement view.

Project economics (revenue 100k, costs 70k, profit 30k) never change — the intercompany tag ensures those numbers stay clean.
